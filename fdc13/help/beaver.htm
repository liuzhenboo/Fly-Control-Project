The system Beaver

The system Beaver is the central non-linear aircraft model from the FDC toolbox. Typing beaver at the command-line will open the first level of the aircraft model, which fulfils the I/O functions for the system. This level will be referred to as Level 1 in the on-line help texts. The actual non-linear aircraft model has been stored in the subsystem Beaver dynamics and output equations, which will be referred to as Level 2 in the on-line help texts.

See also the lists of inputs and outputs to/from the system Beaver, and the color definitions for the FDC systems.
