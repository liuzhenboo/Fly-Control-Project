The autopilot subsystem Computational Delay and Limiters

Computational delay & limiters is a subsystem of the autopilot simulation systems APILOT1, APILOT2, and APILOT3. It determines the computational delay within the Flight Control Computer of the aircraft and the 10 Volt limitation of its output signals. Note that all signals between the control laws and the block Actuator and cable dynamics, including the signals within the subsystem Computational delay & limiters have been expressed in Volts, whereas the outputs of the block Actuator and cable dynamics represent changes in elevator-deflection, aileron-deflection, and rudder-deflection, respectively, which consequently have been measured in [rad].
